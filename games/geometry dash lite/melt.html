The rules of Geometry Dash Meltdown

Play Geometry Dash Meltdown to explore Seven Seas, Viking Arena, and Airborne Robots. Avoid hitting obstacles and collect 3 coins along the way.

A cube character starts a new adventure. At this time, his destinations are Seven Seas, Viking Arena, and Airborne Robots which are beautiful lands. In these lands, the character has to encounter various dangerous obstacles. Assist him in jumping or flying up to evade them. Do your best to collect three coins and get to the end safely.

How to control: Click the left mouse button to jump or fly up.

Playable game modes in Geometry Dash Meltdown

Like Geometry Dash Lite, there are two game modes including Practice Mode and Normal Mode in this game. You can set checkpoints if you play the Practice Mode. In the Normal Mode, there are no checkpoints on the road. Therefore, you will return to the starting point if you collide with obstacles. Choose one of the game modes to play this game now.
